Indian Laws

Home Projects

This is a Quick reference application for Indian Law & Acts. This app comes in handy for students pursuing Law, getting trained at a Police Academy and also lawyers in their professional workplace.

    • Project Details

    • Client: HAYASREE TECHNOLOGIES
    • Date: March 2013
    • Online: Indian Law & Acts

    Project Story

    The user can quickly search the details for a specific Section Number within the Law & Acts by entering the corresponding Section Number. This application contains: 1.Criminal Laws 2.Evidence Act Laws 3.CrPC Laws. Other Features of the App includes: - A detailed listing of the Supreme Court and all the High Courts in India. - A gallery of different courts in India - Calendar stating the national holidays for the current year. - Share feature to send information regarding a specific law to other colleagues or students
